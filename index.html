Features & Innovations

AI-Based Voice Navigation

Step-by-step voice guidance with GPS and AI pathfinding. No smartphone required - built-in navigation system with predefined routes.

Advanced Obstacle Detection

LiDAR sensor scans up to 8 meters ahead, detecting ground-level, mid-level, and high-level obstacles with voice and vibration alerts.

Traffic Light Detection

Beacon-based system detects traffic signals at crossings, providing vibration and voice alerts for safe crossing.

Emergency Response System

Real-time GPS tracking, automatic fall detection, and instant SOS alerts to emergency contacts.

AI Route Optimization

Smart pathfinding that considers traffic, obstacles, and environmental factors for the safest route.

Weather Updates

Real-time weather alerts and advisories to help users prepare for changing conditions.

Live Sound Monitoring

Remote audio monitoring for caregivers to ensure safety and provide real-time assistance.

Haptic Feedback

Vibration-based alerts for deaf-blind users, providing non-verbal navigation assistance.

Ultra-Low Power

Smart sleep mode and long battery life lasting weeks to months based on usage.

IoT Integration

Cloud-based emergency response system for faster assistance and improved security.
